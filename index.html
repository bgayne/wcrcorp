DAYTONA'S ONE-STOP IT RESOURCE

Since 1989, Winchester has been Daytona’s prime stop for computer service and support. We offer full-service computer repair and custom, made-to-order PCs. See why Daytona has trusted Winchester Computers for 29 years.

Your local business solution.

Winchester Computers is locally owned and operated. With decades of combined computer service experience, our technicians are capable of solving all technical problems home users or small- to medium-sized business may encounter. Winchester has built its reputation on customer trust and reliable service.

On-Site Service

Winchester is here for your business. Our support staff can manage the computing needs of small and mid-sized businesses. Since 1989, we’ve been one of Daytona’s premier business support resources. See why Winchester has been Daytona's trusted business support solution for over 29 years.

Network Administration

Our technicians are fully trained to support a modern computer network. We’re capable of setting up basic small office systems as well as full-featured business networks. Call or stop by to schedule a consultation with our network technicians, who can create a plan to address your unique networking needs.

Server Administration

Let our technicians manage your business’ server infrastructure. Trained on Windows and Linux administration, our technicians can deploy and administer most server configurations appropriate for small- and medium-sized businesses.

Workstation Support

Broken hardware? Failed update? We’re here. Winchester has the resources to service your organization’s individual workstations. Need a new PC integrated on your network? Time for an upgrade? We can do it all.

Web Services

An online presence is an essential part of modern business. Winchester’s technical staff is able to get you online with a modern personalized website of your own. We take care of it all: design, development, hosting, and management. Sit back and let us develop your online identity.

Cloud Administration

It’s not just a buzzword. Cloud hosting offers a level of manageability, versatility, and availability that traditional bare-metal servers do not. Your cloud server grows with your business. With cloud technology, we can adapt to your changing business needs.

Web Development & Design

Develop your online identity with a personalized website that uniquely represents your business. Move away from pre-made templates. Let us hand-craft the site that communicates your business to the world. All sites are designed to be mobile-ready. Whether it’s a static landing page or a dynamic web app, we’re here for you.

Managed Services

Move your business infrastructure online. At Winchester, our support staff is capable of creating a modern cloud-based business solution that is tailored to your needs. Let us manage the challenge of updating and maintaining your business’s digital IT needs.

Cloud Hosting

Cloud hosting offers unmatched scalability and availability compared to traditional hosting solutions. As your business grows, we can deploy and manage a cloud solution that grows with your business needs.

Cloud Backups

Regular backups are essential to a modern business’s data security. While traditional onsite backups are adequate, they can be a volatile point of failure. Adding a cloud backup solution to your business’s IT infrastructure ensures that your data is secure and always available.

Productivity and Mail Services

Collaboration is key to a modern business. Managed mail and office services now offer an unmatched opportunity to bring your workplace together. Our support staff has experience deploying and maintaining both Office 365 and G Suites, which offer clients a full-featured collaborative office suite as well as a fully managed mail service.

Workstation Management

Your workstations are the heart of your business’s IT infrastructure. They connect you and your users to the vital resources that keeps your organization running. Here at Winchester, we provide a full-service workstation solution, ranging from custom-built machines to full hardware and software support.

Custom Workstations

All desktop workstations sold by Winchester are built in Daytona Beach Florida. We’re a Microsoft-certified original equipment manufacturer. Every workstation is built to your specifications and can be customized to fit the individual needs of each employee.

Local Support

We’re here for you. If something happens to a workstation in your organization, Winchester is always local and available for you. All workstations are serviced in Daytona Beach by trained technicians. Our business clients receive priority service for all workstation diagnostics and repairs.

Flexible Plans

We work with you to find an affordable solution to your workstation needs. We offer bulk pricing and long-term replacement plans to help fit your budget. With our fully customized workstation solutions, we make sure you’re paying for only what you need, not what we can sell you.

Certified Experts

Our technician team consists of professionals trained and certified to work on Windows-based workstations and Macs in a business environment. Integrating and maintaining individual user workstations on a modern business-focused network can be complicated. We’re to help.
